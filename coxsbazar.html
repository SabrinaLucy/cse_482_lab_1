Favorite Destination

Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar is one of my favorite places because the beach feels calm and refreshing. The long sea beach, sound of waves, and evening sunset make it a special place to visit with family or friends.

Why I Like It

The open sea, beach walks, and sunset views make Cox's Bazar relaxing after busy student life.

Things To Do

Walking on the beach, taking photos, eating seafood, and watching the sunset are the best activities there.

Best Memory

A quiet evening beside the sea is simple, but it stays in memory for a long time.
